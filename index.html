Our Story

Established in the year 2000 by our visionary founder, Sanjay Kumar, Patel Traders started with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring pure, unpolished, and highly durable premium furniture to every household.

For over two decades, we have strictly adhered to our core philosophy. We do not believe in artificial polishing or deceptive masking; our wood speaks for itself. Quality craftsmanship and transparent business practices are the pillars that have earned us the trust of thousands of loyal customers across the nation.
